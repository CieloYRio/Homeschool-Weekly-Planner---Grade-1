📝 Teacher/Facilitator Notes

🎯 Research-Based Timing & Strategy

⏱️ Optimal Session Lengths

  • 20-30 min for Grade 1 core subjects (not 45 min)
  • Shorter sessions prevent cognitive overload
  • Frequent breaks maintain attention
  • Quality focus over quantity time

🔄 Content Subject Strategy

  • Science & Araling: Twice weekly for deeper learning
  • Spelling: Twice weekly for skill reinforcement
  • French: Daily 20-min sessions more effective than weekly
  • Spaced repetition improves retention

✝️ Bible-First Approach

  • Scripture foundation sets the tone for learning
  • Character building before academic building
  • Spiritual wisdom guides all other subjects
  • Creates reverent, focused learning atmosphere

🧮 Math After Bible Strategy

  • Mind is fresh and focused after devotion
  • Logical thinking flows from spiritual grounding
  • Peak concentration for challenging concepts
  • God's order reflected in mathematical patterns

🎯 Interactive Planning

  • Student participates in goal-setting process
  • Weekly targets discussed and agreed upon
  • Child ownership increases motivation
  • Flexible adjustments based on progress

🎹 MAPEH Integration

  • Piano classes count as Music component
  • Open slot ready for PE addition
  • Art can be integrated into other subjects
  • Health topics woven into Science lessons
